Our Story

How we got here

"We mighta took the long way — we knew we'd get there someday."

— our dear old friend Shania Twain

It started with two strangers who had no idea where meeting each other would lead — just a shared love for the sea, the mountains, and the kind of adventures you don't plan in advance.

From the seas and summits of the Philippines — freediving through Apo Reef, Malapascua, and Moalboal, and conquering the peaks of Lanaya, Sirao, and Mt. Apo — we discovered that nothing humbles you quite like nature does. Somewhere along the way, the breathless climbs and the deep dives and all the questionable decisions in between stopped being just adventures. They became our thing.

But then, without either of us really noticing — everything, everywhere, changed all at once.

Three years of long distance, endless video calls, and one very long pandemic. We held on — because some things are simply worth it.

Until we finally found ourselves sharing a home in Singapore, where the real work began: learning each other in the small moments, the ordinary Tuesday evenings, the life we quietly built together.

And then one sun-drenched afternoon in Valldemossa...
at a little village on the island of Mallorca.

At a quiet garden we almost walked past.
A question.
A nervous laugh.
And an excited yes.

And now, here we are.

Turns out the greatest adventure wasn't underwater or up a mountain — who knew it would end up being this?

Two strangers.
Our favourite people.
One adventure of a lifetime.

So dress your best, come hungry, pour the wine, and let's make it a night worth remembering.

We can't wait to celebrate with you.
